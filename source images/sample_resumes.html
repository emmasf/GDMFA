Resumés

Jennifer Cole Phillips

Collected here are a few undergraduate senior, PBac, and GDMFA resumé examples. Resumés are among the most challenging typographic/hierarchical structures as they contain complex information that must be organized clearly and carefully to maximize access and appeal. Pay particular attention to structure, typographic grey values, interval spacing, and tension of the page. Again, edit, revise, and massage the writing and design many times before sending it out.
