The Mission

I felt that people`s neurons seem to be plugged into the digital stream nowadays, having adjusted to the continual barrage of visual information. With multiple windows, scrolling text, personal digital assistants, new media, digital imagery, video on demand, etc, people today have come to appreciate the fact that visuals reduce the time it takes for a viewer to understand and responded to the information. So I wanted to focused on building the interactive experience that was more playful but less informational.

It was impossible to create a real 3d object that transformed from a digital model. But, thanks to the 3D printing technolog today, I could print out my models from real objects. Instead of printing out a normal solid 3d models, I wished to add more mysterious elements so I printed my models with meshed structures.
